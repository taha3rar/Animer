Go ahead and add your clients to your network on Avenews-GT

Do you have clients that you usually do business with? The Clients feature is where you can invite new clients to your network, centrally manage your existing clients, and review the transactions you have with them.

Easily order products directly from your suppliers

Create and distribute purchase orders to your sellers, review their proforma invoices, upload your own necessary documents, and manage the entire trade process in one place. To start a new transaction, simply click on the New Purchase Order button.

Create customized groups of clients

Have a lot of clients? The Ecosystems feature is where you can create customized groups of clients with common characteristics, such as location, product type, operation type and more. To add a new ecosystem, simply start by clicking on the New Ecosystem button.

Receive Proforma Invoices directly from clients

The Proforma Invoices section is where you can review, download, and manage all of the proforma invoices submitted by your sellers in one place. Donâ€™t worry about staying updated - once a proforma invoice has been received, you will be informed with live notifications.

Easily distribute Proforma Invoices directly to your clients

The Invoices feature is where you can generate, review and distribute stand-alone proforma invoices, which are proforma invoices that are not connected directly to a purchase order you received from a buyer on the platform. To generate a new stand-alone proforma invoice, simply click on the New Proforma Invoice button.

Create a digital inventory for your products

The Products feature is where you can create a customizable inventory with digital product profiles that holds key product details such as SKU, certifications, packing options and more. To add a product, simply start by clicking on the Add New Product button.

Receive and reply to purchase orders

The Orders feature is where you can receive purchase orders from your client, reply to them with customized proforma invoices, upload your own necessary documents, and manage the entire trade process in one place. Once a purchase order has been received, you will be notified and then all you need to do is reply with a proforma invoice.
