Welcome to Payments!

Just follow the simple steps to set up the payments feature.

Steps to do to activate payments:

  • Complete KYC form in the settings.

    • Your info has been sent to DPO and is being processed.

    You will be contacted by DPO within 48 hours to complete the process.

  • Send request to top-up your balance.

    • Your request has been sent and a DPO agent will be in touch with you soon to complete the process.
