Welcome to Products!

The Products feature is where you can create a customizable inventory with digital product profiles that holds key product details such as SKU, certifications, packing options and more. To add a product, simply start by clicking on the Add Product button and choose between:

  • Processed product - products such as jams, juices, paste,etc.
  • Agricultural product - raw products such as beans, grains, fruits, vegetables, etc.
close

New Product

Adding a new product is composed of 4 simple steps:

  • Pick a buyer
  • Add product details
  • Add pricing details.
  • Review and send
close

What else?

When you generate a product profile, your buyer can see it and order it directly from your inventory. In addition, to review your product profiles, simply click on the View button in the relevant product row.
