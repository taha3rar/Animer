Welcome to Contacts !

The Contacts feature is where you can invite new contact to your network, centrally manage your existing contact, and review the transactions you have with them. To add a new contact, simply start by clicking on the Add a Contact button.

New Contact

Adding a new contact is composed of 4 simple steps:

  • Add contact details
  • Add company details
  • Add the contact to an ecosystem (A customized group of contacts)
  • Review and submit

At the end of each step, click on the Next button to proceed.
