Welcome to Clients!

The Clients feature is where you can invite new clients to your network, centrally manage your existing clients, and review the transactions you have with them. To add a new client, simply start by clicking on the Add Client button.

New Client

Adding a new client is composed of 4 simple steps:

  • Add contact details
  • Add company details
  • Add the client to an ecosystem (A customized group of clients)
  • Review and submit

At the end of each step, click on the Next button to proceed.
