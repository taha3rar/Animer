Welcome to Proforma Invoices!

The Proforma Invoices feature is where you can generate, review and distribute stand-alone proforma invoices, which are proforma invoices that are not connected directly to a purchase order you received from a buyer on the platform. To generate a new stand-alone proforma invoice, simply click on the New Proforma Invoice button.

close

New Proforma Invoice

Issuing a stand-alone proforma invoice is composed of 3 simple steps:

  • Pick a buyer
  • Customize the proforma invoice by adding your contact details, product specifications, terms & conditions and more.
  • Review the invoice and send it directly to your buyer.

At the end of each step, click on the Next button to proceed.

close

What's next?

When you generate a proforma invoice, the buyer will be notified automatically. In addition, to review the proforma invoice you’ve generated, simply click on the View button in the relevant row, and it will lead you to the issued proforma invoice which you can also download as PDF.

Welcome to Proforma Invoices!

The proforma Invoices section is where you can review, download, and manage all of the proforma invoices submitted by your sellers in one place. Don’t worry about staying updated - once an proforma invoice has been received, you will be informed with live notifications. In order to review the received proforma invoices generated by the seller, simply click on the View button in the relevant row and it will lead you to the issued proforma invoice which you can also download as PDF.
